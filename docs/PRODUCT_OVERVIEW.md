# Product Overview

## Vision

FelekiDB is a social movie-night planner that transforms the often chaotic process of choosing what to watch into a fun, democratic experience. By combining voting mechanics with a reputation system, we create accountability and gamification around movie selection.

## Problem Statement

Choosing a movie with a group is surprisingly difficult:
- Everyone has different preferences
- Decision paralysis leads to wasted time
- No one remembers who picked what
- No accountability for bad picks
- Scheduling is done through fragmented chat messages

## Solution

FelekiDB provides a structured workflow for movie nights:

1. **Create Event** - Host picks a date and gives the night a name
2. **Invite Friends** - Share a link or invite by username
3. **Nominate Movies** - Everyone adds movies they want to watch
4. **Vote** - Democratic selection from the nominations
5. **Watch Together** - The movie with most votes wins
6. **Rate** - Everyone rates the movie after watching
7. **Reputation Update** - The nominator's score is affected

## Target Users

### Primary: Friend Groups (Ages 18-35)
- Regular movie night attendees
- 4-8 people per group
- Mix of in-person and virtual watch parties

### Secondary: Film Clubs
- Organized viewing groups
- Thematic movie selections
- Discussion-focused communities

## Key Value Propositions

| Value | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Democratic Selection** | Everyone gets a voice through nominations and voting |
| **Accountability** | Reputation system tracks who picks great (or terrible) movies |
| **Organization** | Calendar, invites, and history in one place |
| **Discovery** | TMDB integration exposes users to new movies |
| **Fun Competition** | Leaderboards and reputation create friendly rivalry |

## Success Metrics

### Engagement
- Active users per week
- Movie nights created per user
- Average group size
- Completion rate (created → watched → rated)

### Retention
- Weekly retention rate
- Month-1 and Month-3 retention
- Returning hosts (users who create multiple nights)

### Quality
- Average session duration
- Voting participation rate
- Rating submission rate

## Non-Goals (MVP)

The following are explicitly out of scope for MVP:
- Real-time chat during movie watching
- Direct streaming integration
- Payment splitting
- Public/discoverable movie nights
- Mobile native apps

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Gap FelekiDB Fills |
|------------|-------------------|
| General polls (Doodle) | No movie-specific features |
| Movie trackers (Letterboxd) | Single-user focused, no group voting |
| Watch party apps (Teleparty) | No selection or reputation |
| Group chats | No structure, lost history |

FelekiDB combines the best of scheduling, movie discovery, and social accountability into one focused product.
